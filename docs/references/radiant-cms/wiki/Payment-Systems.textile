If you wish for your site to have a shop or subscription system then there are a few options available.

h2. E-commerce

h3. Rbiz

"Rbiz":http://github.com/rubidine/rbiz is an e-commerce Rails plugin for building shopping cart systems. A "Radiant extension":http://github.com/rubidine/radiant_rbiz integrates Rbiz into your Radiant installation, making products, product categories, and the cart checkout process available to your Radiant pages.

h2. Subscription Systems.

h3. Spreedly

"http://www.spreedly.com":http://www.spreedly.com is a site which manages subscriptions for you. There is also a "Radiant extension":http://github.com/pelargir/radiant-spreedly-extension/tree/master that more tightly integrates management into your radiant admin system
